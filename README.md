# Publications

The Global AI Liability Consortium advances AI liability through publications that communicate enduring knowledge to organizations, professionals, researchers, policymakers, and the public.

GAILC publications translate research, institutional experience, and professional practice into resources that strengthen understanding and advance AI accountability.

Together, these publications contribute to the world's enduring knowledge infrastructure for AI liability.

> **Liability is where AI disciplines converge.**

---

# Purpose

The Publications Program exists to communicate authoritative, practical, and enduring knowledge across the global AI liability community.

Publications preserve institutional knowledge, support professional practice, encourage informed decision-making, and promote interdisciplinary understanding of AI liability.

Each publication contributes to GAILC's broader mission of advancing AI accountability through knowledge.

---

# Publication Philosophy

GAILC publications are developed as enduring institutional resources.

They are intended to remain valuable beyond individual technologies, regulations, organizations, or market cycles.

Every publication should contribute lasting knowledge that strengthens the global understanding and practice of AI liability.
